Meet Keith Blalock

Coaching that helps you see clearly and move decisively.

KB Coaching is built for people who know something needs to change and want trusted support to create that change with confidence.

Hi, I’m Keith Blalock, a certified life and business coach based in Houma, Louisiana. I help people get unstuck, gain clarity, and create real momentum in their personal and professional lives.

My coaching style is direct, honest, and practical. I support clients who want to overcome challenges, set focused goals, rebuild confidence, improve mindset and accountability, or find steadier footing in overwhelming seasons.

Outside of coaching, I’m also a photographer and automotive enthusiast. Creativity, perspective, and growth all shape the way I coach, and I believe meaningful progress happens when clarity meets action.
